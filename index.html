About the Game

"Hobby Farm Show" lets players build and manage their own farm, growing crops, raising animals, and crafting products. With diverse gameplay that combines farming, strategy, and resource management, this game offers hours of fun as players level up, unlock new plants and animals, and complete exciting challenges. The game is designed to be both engaging and relaxing, making it a favorite for all ages.

How to Play

  1. 1. Plant and Harvest Crops: Choose from a variety of plants, each with unique growth times and benefits.

  2. 2. Raise Animals: Care for animals and use their produce to create goods.

  3. 3. Raise Animals: Build new structures, unlock new lands, and explore different farming zones

  4. 4. Complete Tasks: Fulfill orders, tackle quests, and manage resources to earn rewards.

  5. 5. Earn Rewards: Gather coins and special items to upgrade your farm and make it thrive.
